Kingswood research unit is one of the most important societies within the Kingswood circle. Nurturing our young Kingswood gentlemen to become the next generation of industry leaders in the world of research is our sworn duty. It is with this in mind that we go about our duties and projects. The research unit of Kingswood College, as one of the first ever school-based research programmes in Sri Lanka is a pioneer and an innovator in school research. Extending our support to aspiring researchers both within and out of the school limits we have had massive success in the short history of the club in building a Research culture within the school community. Our past projects have brought together researchers of many different pedigrees to culminate a positive and forward-thinking group of young innovators and possibly some of Sri Lanka’s future greatest minds. Headed today, by Kingswood gentleman's Kavira Rathnayake & Dasun Perera under the watchful eye of our staff counsellor Mrs. Erandi Ranasinghe and Principal, Mr. Namal Chandana Kumara the Kingswood Research unit is the legacy of the inaugural committee. Our plans for this year include ‘APIERO’, An inter-school webinar series on Research, ‘Ingratus’, An annual joint research fair and symposium held in conjunction with the Research units of KGHS and MGCK, ‘Conspicio’, An inter-school workshop and ‘Experzio’, Annual research day and installation ceremony. We also hope to initiate and publish a research journal under the unit, to legitimize the valuable research work of our young Kingswood gentlemen.
